Our Services

Afforestation

Vasundhara actively promotes environmental conservation and restoration, focusing on afforestation, biodiversity preservation, and sustainable land management.

Through community engagement, the NGO works to restore natural ecosystems, ensuring a sustainable environment for future generations.

Afforestation

Jal Sangrahan

Vasundhara prioritizes sustainable water management through community-based initiatives, focusing on rainwater harvesting and watershed management to enhance water quality and availability.

The NGO's efforts mitigate water scarcity and promote environmental resilience.

Jal Sangrahan

Tikakaran

Vasundhara arranges vaccination camps in rural areas, providing essential immunizations, especially for newborns, protecting against diseases like polio.

Focusing on accessible healthcare, the NGO safeguards the well-being of remote communities, particularly vulnerable populations.

Tikakaran

Medical Camps

Vasundhara organizes regular medical camps in rural areas, offering general check-ups and treatments while emphasizing health awareness and hygiene education.

The NGO empowers communities with essential knowledge to prevent diseases and enhance overall well-being.

Medical Camps

Workshop & Trainings

Vasundhara conducts STEM workshops and trains tutors to integrate new technologies and innovative teaching methods, empowering educators to prepare students for opportunities in STEM fields.

Workshop & Trainings

Diverse abilities assistance

Vasundhara provides specialized care and education for children with diverse needs and disabilities, fostering an inclusive and supportive environment that promotes their well-being and overall development.

Diverse abilities & assistance
